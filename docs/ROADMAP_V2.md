\# Long-Term Goal



MentorX should become a platform that allows any learner to:



\- Choose a career domain

\- Generate a personalized roadmap

\- Study daily

\- Practice quizzes

\- Chat with an AI mentor

\- Attend AI mock interviews

\- Analyze resumes

\- Track skills

\- Become job-ready



\---



\# Success Criteria



A user should be able to start with zero knowledge in a domain and use MentorX as their primary learning and interview preparation platform until they achieve their career goal.



\*\*Target Users\*\*



\- Students

\- Fresh Graduates

\- Career Switchers

\- Working Professionals

\- Self-Learners

\- Technical Interview Candidates
